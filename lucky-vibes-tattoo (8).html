Who We Are

Laconia's Home

Lucky Vibes Tattoo & Body Piercing is a local tattoo studio proudly serving the Lakes Region of New Hampshire. We specialize in custom tattoos and professional body piercings in a friendly, clean, and welcoming environment.

Whether it's your first tattoo or your twentieth, our artists take the time to listen, collaborate, and create artwork that is truly one of a kind — craftsmanship you'll be proud to wear for life.
